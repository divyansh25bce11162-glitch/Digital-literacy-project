## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone
- Do not approve unknown UPI collect/payment requests
- Always verify the sender before making any transaction
- Use only official and trusted payment apps
- Enable two-factor authentication on accounts
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
- Regularly check bank statements for unauthorized activity
- Do not install apps from unknown sources

### UPI Safety Tips
- Remember: receiving money does NOT require entering UPI PIN
- Decline unexpected “collect request” transactions

### Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

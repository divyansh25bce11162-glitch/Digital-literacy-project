## Social Media Do's and Don'ts

### Do's
- Use professional language in posts
- Keep your profiles clean and respectful
- Protect your personal information
- Verify information before sharing
- Use privacy settings properly

### Don'ts
- Do not share passwords or sensitive data
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not spread misinformation
- Avoid oversharing personal life details
- Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
